Voting

  1. Only citizens can vote

    1. Green-hard holders can’t vote until they become citizens
    2. Even if you have a driver’s license/some form of ID, you can’t vote unless you are a citizen.
    3. Only citizens can register to vote!

  2. Things You Should Not Do

    1. Do not vote if you aren’t a citizen. This can result in your naturalization application being denied. In some cases, you could be deported by ICE.

  3. How To Resolve Voting Mistakes

    1. If you accidentally register to vote...

      1. Contact the County Auditor’s office and ask them to cancel your registration.

    2. Contact an immigration lawyer. cClifornia provides free legal consultation.
